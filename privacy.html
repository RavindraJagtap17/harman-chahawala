Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 April 2026

Harman Chahawala ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect personal information when you visit www.harmanchahawala.com or submit a franchise enquiry to us.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit our enquiry form or contact us, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • City and state of interest
  • Investment range and franchise model preference
  • Message or additional notes you provide

We may also automatically collect non-personal data such as browser type, device information, IP address (in anonymised form), and pages visited, via standard server logs and basic analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your franchise enquiry and share business proposal documents
  • To verify your eligibility for a Harman Chahawala franchise
  • To send updates, offers, and information about our brand and outlets
  • To improve our website, products, and franchise programs
  • To comply with applicable laws and prevent fraud or misuse

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data only with:

  • Authorised team members handling franchise enquiries
  • Service providers (e.g., email delivery, hosting) bound by confidentiality
  • Government authorities when required by law

4. Cookies

Our site may use small cookie files to remember preferences and gather aggregate visitor analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some functionality may be reduced.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your information, including HTTPS encryption, captcha-protected forms, server-side validation, and limited internal access. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing info@harmanchahawala.com. You may also opt out of marketing communications by replying with "Unsubscribe".

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (e.g., social media, Google Maps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their policies separately.

8. Children

Our services are intended for adults aged 18+ exploring entrepreneurship. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.
