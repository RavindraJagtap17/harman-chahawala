From Streets To Steaming Cups

The Heart Behind Harman Chahawala

"Where life threw challenges, Hari brewed hope. Every cup carries 32 years of dedication."

Haribhau Kulkarni lost his father at just two years old, leaving his family to survive the unforgiving streets of Sangli. Born in hardship, raised by resilience — at 22, he began selling tea not just as a livelihood, but as a dream in the making.

In 2018, with his son Siddharth by his side, he founded Harman Chahawala — a brand steeped in struggle, strength, and the spirit of self-belief. From a roadside stall to a respected name in Indian tea, our 32-year journey is more than business — it's a story of grit, grace, and greatness in every cup.
