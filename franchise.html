Choose The Franchise Model That Fits You

Two flexible models built for different entrepreneur profiles. Both are based on a 150 sq. ft. outlet — costs may vary based on shop size.

Option 1
"Plug N Play" Franchise Model

Complete turnkey solution — we set everything up so you can launch quickly without any operational hassles.

  • Full furniture & interior work
  • Steel fixtures, counters & commercial gas range
  • Complete kitchen utensil setup
  • Branded uniforms, cups & LED name board
  • All electrical appliances included
  • Ready-to-operate outlet handover
Option 2
"Do It Yourself" Franchise Model

Ideal for budget-conscious partners who want more control over setup and sourcing — at company-approved standards.

  • Franchisee manages furniture & steel fixtures
  • Franchisee sources kitchen & electrical appliances
  • All items must match company-approved standards
  • Branding done exclusively by Harman's in-house vendor
  • Lower upfront investment
  • Same brand, supply chain and training support

* Applicable for a 150 sq. ft. outlet. Costs may vary based on shop size and location.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost of a Harman Chahawala tea franchise?

Harman Chahawala offers two franchise models — Plug N Play and Do It Yourself — applicable for a 150 sq. ft. outlet. The exact cost depends on shop size and chosen model. Connect with us at +91 91171 51715 or info@harmanchahawala.com for a personalised proposal.

Is there a royalty fee in the franchise?

No. Harman Chahawala operates on a 100% royalty-free model — franchise partners keep all earnings beyond their operational costs.

How much can I earn from a franchise?

Earnings depend on daily cup volume. Selling 900 cups/day yields approximately ₹1,13,670 monthly profit; 1200 cups/day yields ₹1,62,800; and 1500 cups/day yields up to ₹2,85,590 in monthly profit (figures inclusive of additional add-on income).

Where is the franchise available?

We currently offer franchise opportunities across Maharashtra, Gujarat, Goa and Madhya Pradesh, with plans to expand pan-India.

What space do I need for an outlet?

A minimum 150 sq. ft. carpet area in a high-footfall location is required. You'll also need Shop Act, FSSAI and GST registrations and 2-4 staff members.
